The Technique

How SMP Works

Our 3D layering technique builds realism across multiple sessions — not a single flat pass.

Consultation & Design

We review your hair-loss pattern, scalp type, and goals, then design a hairline tailored to your face, age, and features.

Foundation Layer

Using micro-fine needles, a first layer of lighter-tone pigment impressions is placed to form the base follicle field.

3D Depth & Refinement

Over 2–3 sessions we layer darker tones to create a realistic 3D depth that reads as real follicles on a 2D scalp.
